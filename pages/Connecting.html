Connecting to the Internet


We've spoken much about the Internet and how it was created. But how does one actually connect to the Internet? This page will explain just that!

Connecting to the Internet

There are two ways of getting the internet. The most popular way is to install a high capacity connection in your home called broadband. You can also connect to the internet on a smartphone, using your mobile company�s internet service.

Next we�ll explain how to get the internet at home and how to connect your computer to it, using a landline.

You will need:

A modern laptop, desktop computer, smartphone or tablet

A phone line.

Choosing an Internet Service Provider (ISP)

An Internet Service Provider is the company that provides you with an internet connection. In most cases, people tend to use the same company to provide their phone line and their internet connection. You could always ask a friend or relative for help if you�re unsure who to choose.

Wait for your router to arrive

A router is a small device you use to connect to the internet. Once you�ve ordered an internet connection, a router will be sent out to your home. It will also arrive with step-by-step instructions to help you set it all up.

Some companies offer a home installation service and, for an extra cost, will send an engineer to set up your internet connection for you.

Connect to the internet � Wifi

Connect to the internet using either wifi or an Ethernet cable.

To connect using wifi:

Select the wifi icon on your device and then click on your broadband connection. Next you need to enter the password that came with your router. Ask a friend or relative for help if you get stuck.

Connect to the internet � Ethernet

Connect to the internet using either wifi or an Ethernet cable.

To connect using an Ethernet cable:

Plug one end of an Ethernet cable into the modem, and plug the other end into a networking port on the computer that you want to connect to the internet.
